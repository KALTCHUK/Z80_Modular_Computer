Functions for PLC programming

*** Define Networks ***
Syntax:		char defNet(char port, char *baud)
Example:	char net1;
			net1 = defNet( 0x40, "19200");


*** Define Tags - Modbus devices (sensors and actuators) ***
Syntax:		int defTag(char network, char slave, int register)
Example:	#define Amux	5
			int TT_20;
			TT_20 = defTag( net1, Amux, 40120);

*** Read from Device ***
Syntax:		int readTag(int tag)
Example:	if(readTag(TT_20) > 2000) {...

*** Write to Device ***
Syntax:		void writeTag(int tag, int value)
Example:	writeTag(M1, readTag(LT_01)/2);

Example:	screen("p:turbo2.scr");
