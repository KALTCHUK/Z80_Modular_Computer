ATmega328 pin use for USART v2

          7     6     5     4     3     2     1     0
PORT B	XTAL2 XTAL1  SCK  MISO  MOSI   D02   D01   D00

PORT C  ----- RESET   nc   nc    RSM   WR    RD    A01

PORT D   D07   D06   D05   D04   D03   CS    TX    RX


RESET, RSM, WR, RD and CS are all active low signals.
